Donation Impact

ineedtotalk.org provides free support group services to anyone in the world. However, there are a lot of moving pieces involved, ranging from server uptime, to encryption costs, to content creation, and graphic design.

Your donation will go towards sustaining our support group services, and allow the business to expand. Future plans include:

  • iOS and Android applications
  • A database for local crisis centers and shelters
  • Private messaging
  • Audio/video integration
  • Case work services
  • Mobile crisis units
  • Internationalization
