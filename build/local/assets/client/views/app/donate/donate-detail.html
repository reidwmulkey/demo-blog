Donation Impact

ineedtotalk.org provides free support group services to anyone in the world. However, there are a lot of moving pieces involved, ranging from server uptime, to encryption costs, to content creation, and graphic design.

Your donation will go towards sustaining our support group services, and allow the business to expand. Future plans include:

  • iOS and Android applications
  • A database for local crisis centers and shelters
  • Private messaging
  • Audio/video integration
  • Case work services
  • Mobile crisis units
  • Internationalization

In order to leave a donation, You may click the PayPal button below.

Dedication

Any donation of at least {{vm.dedicationAmount}} {{vm.dedicationCurrency}}  will allow you to dedicate your donation to a loved one. All dedications can be viewed here.  If you would like to leave a dedication, then leave your email address as a note in your donation.

Please note: you must use an email address associated with an ineedtotalk account in order to leave a dedication. If you need to modify the email address of your dedication, you may contact  support@ineedtotalk.org. After completing your donation, you will receive an email with details on how to create a dedication.
